About Us

Welcome all readers to my Star Wars e-magazine. I am Ethan Huston a student at EIT and the creator of this website and all the entries into it, excluding images which are owned and credited to other people/companies.

I am a massive Star Wars fan and always have been since a very young age when I fell in love with the prequels. When presented with this assignment to create a website I immediatley wanted to something on the geekier side and when E-magazine came forward as an option, I new that Star Wars would be a perfect fit for the topic. I really wanted to share the love for the topic and this really was the best opportunity, where I can review, show upcoming features and express my love for the subject to others who may also be interested in the topic.

If you ever want to contact me regarding anything on the website, please contact me at ethanthuston67@gmail.com but please keep in mind that I am a student and will not be replying as often as other may.
